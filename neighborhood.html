Highland Park

I am currently in the Highland Park neighborhood in St. Paul, MN. The neighborhood is filled with family homes and friendly people. Everyone here seems to love their homes and especially their gardens. You can always counts on walking your dog and have conversations with a handful of neighbors along the way.

The many great local restaurants and breweries within biking distance make for some very memorable nights non-winter nights. Being located in Minnesota, there are also a few different outdoor rinks within the neighborhood to almost everybody spends all waking hours at. This neighorhod loves hockey and getting out the the neighborhood parks. After everyone is done freezing their butts off in the winter, neighbors all hangout again for the evening at one of the great restaurants.
